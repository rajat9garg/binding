## Mission
To revolutionize online commerce by creating the most trusted and efficient bidding platform that empowers both buyers and sellers through innovative technology, transparent processes, and exceptional user experiences.

## Vision
By 2027, we aim to be the global leader in online auctions, serving 10 million users across 50+ countries, while maintaining our commitment to security, transparency, and user satisfaction. Our platform will be synonymous with trust, innovation, and value in the online marketplace.
